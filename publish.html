Publishing

WHY PUBLISH WITH US?

Here at Adam Games, we are still but a small company. But if you publish with us, we promise to deliver on these 4 fundamentals of Indie game development.

  1. Exposure: Adam Games has an established brand and reputation in the gaming industry, which can help to increase exposure for a developer's game. We also provide advertisement for games published with us.
  2. Support: Adam Games may offer support to developers in terms of marketing, funding, and technical assistance, which helps to reduce the burden on developers and ensure the game's success.
  3. Collaboration: Adam Games offers developers the opportunity to collaborate with other developers within the Adam Games ecosystem, which can lead to new ideas and innovations in game development.
  4. Resources: Adam Games has access to resources that individual developers may not, such as specialized equipment, software, and talent, which can improve the quality of your final product.
  5. Adam: If you work for Adam Games, you are entitled to the privaliage of meeting with Adam himself. With this experience, you will gain a deep appretiation of the universe itself as you bask in his glory

GAME REQUIREMENTS

Now that we've gotten you interested in publishing with us, it's important we establish some ground rules on what we want.

WHAT WE WANT

  • Originality
  • Substance
  • Passion & artistry
  • A clear, strong vision
  • Fun

WHAT WE DON'T WANT

  • Plagiarism
  • Cash grabs
  • NFT's and cryptocurrency
  • Lack of vision
  • Boredom
